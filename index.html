SELF-AIR: Supporting Extensive Livestock Farming with the use of Autonomous Intelligent Robots

Apoyo a la Digitalización de la Ganadería Extensiva con Robot Autónomos Inteligentes

TED2021-132356B-I00

The main goal of the SELF-AIR project is to analyze the use of autonomous legged robots in extensive sheep farming, develop the software required for these tasks, evaluate its performance, and assess the welfare and behavior of the cattle, as well as the wildlife, to the continuous presence of robots. The robots will help in the monitorization, grazing, and as possible deterrents in the event of wolves or feral dogs attacks.
